## CONTAMINATION HISTORY & TRENDS

### 1. TRICHLOROETHYLENE (TCE) - **PRIMARY CONCERN**

| Year | Concentration | Unit | Severity Index | Status |
|------|----------------|------|-----------------|--------|
| 1999 | 5.2 | ppb | 4 (Moderate) | Detected |
| 2004 | 20.6 | ppb | 5 (Elevated) | **↑ Increase** |
| 2006 | 31.8 | ppb | 6 (High) | **↑ Peak** |
| 2008 | 27.7 | ppb | 6 (High) | Stable |

**Trend Analysis**:
- **Slope**: +2.84 ppb/year
- **R² (Correlation)**: 0.8782 (Strong)
- **Classification**: ⚠️ **INCREASING TREND**
- **Interpretation**: TCE concentration shows consistent upward trend from 1999-2008. Peak detected in 2006 (31.8 ppb) with slight stabilization by 2008. Indicates ongoing or recent contamination source.

**Likely Sources**:
- Solvent use in Chemical Manufacturing Facility A (I-001)
- Degreasing/cleaning operations
- Historical spill or slow-release source

**Risk Assessment**: MODERATE-HIGH for this parameter

## FORENSIC ANALYSIS

### Contamination Profile Signature:
**Pattern Detected**: TCE + chlorinated compounds (1,2-DCA, chloroform)
- **Source Signature**: Organic chemical synthesis operations
- **Industrial Match**: Facility I-001 (Chemical Manufacturing A)
- **Confidence Level**: HIGH (location, contaminant combination, industrial type all align)

### Co-occurrence Analysis:
- TCE and 1,2-DCA both elevated in 1999 → suggests common source
- TCE increasing while 1,2-DCA decreases → suggests different mobilization rates or source changes
- Chloroform stable → older background contamination, possibly different source or period

### Temporal Interpretation:
- **1999-2004**: Initial phase - high 1,2-DCA (100 ppb) declining
- **2004-2006**: Transition phase - TCE accelerating, 1,2-DCA spike
- **2006-2008**: Stabilization phase - both contaminants plateauing

### Hypothesis:
Primary source is industrial chemical operations (I-001) with TCE as primary product. The 2006 spike may indicate:
- Remediation mobilizing subsurface contamination
- Seasonal/hydrogeological changes affecting plume migration
- New release event from facility
